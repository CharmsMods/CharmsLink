Cream Mod

Hey, I'm Darynn, but I go by Charm? online. You might have seen me on YouTube playing some games or making edits of those games.

My journey started when I discovered Venge.io, a niche browser shooter game with a Discord server. For most of the time in my life that I've had a PC, I've been extremely passionate about shooter games, and when I found Venge.io, I fell in love with the community and the game.

I contributed to the community by making the only website for automatic modding of the game, where you could upload assets and textures, or make them on the site and export them as a mod, compatible with both browser and client at the same time! I have made several of my own mods, "Cream Mod" has become a community favorite for it's style and performance.

I recently was recognized for my work by way of receiving Verification, a rarely given out position to recognize great contribution to the game and community.

After a while, I moved on from consistently playing Venge and keeping up on the modding site and community, to playing other shooter games. I have loved many of these games for different reasons, but a few stand out to me. I have immensely enjoyed playing The FINALS and another extremely niche game called Banana Shooter.

Meanwhile, while playing shooter games, I have begun to grow an online community through YouTube, Instagram, Discord, and more recently, Twitch. I continue to improve my aim and upgrade my setup.

Over the past year, I have created this entire complex of websites for you to enjoy as much as you want. I have a strong passion for all things visual, so the tools I create are dedicated to providing you with the highest-quality output, free, compared to something paid or a sketchy site online (this isn't one, I promise, you can find all the source code on github). Thanks for being here, I'm always updating this site with new tools and features, so check back often!
